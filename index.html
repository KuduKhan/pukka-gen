How We Work

The Pukka Protocol

This section answers the client's unspoken question: "Am I going to lose money on this?" It establishes a structured, transparent process.

Phase 1

No-Duff Discovery | Consultation

"We don’t start coding until we understand the 'Why.'"

What we do: We sit down with you to strip away the fluff. We identify the core business problem, the target audience, and the technical constraints.

The Output: A Project Scope Document that outlines exactly what will be delivered—no hidden costs, no vague promises.
01
02
Phase 2

The Blueprint | UI/UX Design

"Measure twice, cut once."

What we do: We create wireframes and high-fidelity mockups. You will see exactly what your app or site will look like before we write a single line of logic. This minimizes revisions later.

The Output: Interactive Prototypes and Design Systems.
Phase 3

The Build | Agile Dev

"Solid engineering, delivered in sprints."

What we do: We work in 2-week cycles (sprints). At the end of every cycle, you get a demo. You are never left in the dark wondering if work is happening.

The Output: A staging link where you can watch your software come to life in real-time.
03
04
Phase 4

Fortification | QA & Security

"If it’s not Pukka, it doesn’t ship."

What we do: We stress-test the system. We try to break it so your users can’t. We test for speed, security vulnerabilities, and mobile responsiveness.

The Output: A Bug-Free Release Candidate.
Phase 5

Deployment | DevOps & Support

"Launch day is just Day 1."

What we do: We manage the server setup and domain configuration. After launch, we offer maintenance packages to keep the software updated and secure.

The Output: A secure, live digital product with ongoing support.
